01 — Measure

Where is the spend going?

Cost by model, customer, feature, workflow, and provider. Per-request timeline fused with engine /metrics and DCGM. A baseline you can defend in a board meeting.

02 — Diagnose

Why is it expensive?

Premium-model overuse, RAG context bloat, agent loops and retries, missed caching, customer margin leak, KV-cache pressure, wrong runtime, wrong hardware. Ranked by expected savings and risk.

03 — Optimize

What ships?

Better prompting. Smarter RAG. Aggressive caching. Cheaper-model routing. Provider switching. Eval gates that prove savings did not break quality. When the math says hybrid or self-host wins, we help you move there safely. Partners come in only when the bottleneck is theirs.
