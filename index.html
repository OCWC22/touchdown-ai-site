About Touchdown Labs

Touchdown Labs focuses on inference optimization, self-hosting, technical education, research, and services. We work on practical problems around inference performance, deployment decisions, and the infrastructure required to run modern AI systems well.

Our public work includes open benchmarks, datasets, and tooling that contribute useful signal to the broader ecosystem. Those efforts support operators today while also reflecting the problems we care about most deeply.

We care about making advanced AI systems more understandable, more practical, and easier to operate outside closed platforms.

Long term, we want to go beyond software into designing and manufacturing silicon in the United States and helping build the data centers that support it.
